## app/controllers/ <a name = "app_controllers"></a>

Handles HTTP requests.

When hit they render views or return JSON. Named for their route location.

E.g. PagesController (`app/controllers/pages_controller.rb`) decides what happens when someone visits `/pages/`.

Aka, the front line staff of our server.
